The Detection-First Doctrine

Unlike standard CLI tools that force a state upon your repository, Git-Flow Skill prioritizes environmental awareness. It scans existing branch structures, commit histories, and remote states before suggesting a flow. This ensures your agent-based workflows remain stable, predictable, and historically accurate—preserving the integrity of your technical ledger.
